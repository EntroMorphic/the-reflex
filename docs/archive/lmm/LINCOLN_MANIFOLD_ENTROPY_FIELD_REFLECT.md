## Unpacking

Traditional computing treats information as **structure**—the bits, the signals, the shapes. Entropy is noise to be minimized, error to be corrected.

The insight: **computation is the flow from high entropy to low entropy**. From uncertainty to certainty. From possibility to actuality. From void to shape.

We're not computing ON the entropy. The entropy flow IS the computation.
