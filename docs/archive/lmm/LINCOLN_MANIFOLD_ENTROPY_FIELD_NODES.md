## Core Nodes

### 1. **Void as Information**
The space between signals carries information. Silence is not absence of data—it's data about absence.

### 2. **Entropy Accumulation**
Time without signal = entropy accumulates. The longer the silence, the more potential energy in the void.

### 3. **Crystallization Threshold**
When entropy exceeds capacity, void collapses into structure. Phase transition from potential to actual.

### 4. **Entropy Gradient**
Direction of entropy flow. Information flows from high entropy (uncertainty) to low entropy (structure).

### 5. **Stigmergy**
Indirect communication through the environment. Shapes leave traces in the void. Void carries the message.

### 6. **Diffusion**
Entropy spreads to neighbors. Local concentrations disperse over time. The field equilibrates.

### 7. **Decay**
Entropy slowly dissipates. Without replenishment, the void returns to baseline. Memory fades.

### 8. **Void Cell**
Atomic unit of the entropy field. Has entropy level, capacity, gradient, and state.

### 9. **Entropy Field**
Grid of void cells. The computational substrate. Where shapes exist and interact.

### 10. **Shape-Void Duality**
Shape = low entropy = structure. Void = high entropy = potential. Each defines the other.
