   The Open Core Model for The Reflex:

   Layer                         │ License        │ What's Included                                                              
   ------------------------------+----------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   **Core Primitives**           │ MIT/Apache 2.0 │ `reflex.h`, channel primitive, basic CfC Reflexor, entropy field fundamentals
   **Reference Implementations** │ MIT/Apache 2.0 │ ESP32-C6 demos, Pi4 benchmarks, research experiments
   **Enterprise Extensions**     │ Commercial     │ Reflexor-as-shape, Hebbian attention, multi-agent stigmergy, certified builds
   **Platform Services**         │ SaaS           │ Fleet telemetry, anomaly dashboards, remote vitality monitoring

   Why this structure:

   Benefit               │ Rationale                                                                                                                      
   ----------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   **Adoption flywheel** │ Open core gets into research labs, startups, education. They become advocates and future enterprise customers
   **Patent defense**    │ Open-sourcing the primitives creates prior art. Harder for competitors to patent cache-coherency coordination
   **Community R&D**     │ Academics extend the entropy field, discover new phenomena, publish papers. Free research
   **Enterprise moat**   │ Self-organizing Reflexors, certified builds for safety-critical (DO-178C, IEC 61508) are hard to replicate and worth paying for

   Revenue streams:

   1. Enterprise licenses - Reflexor-as-shape, Hebbian routes, multi-node coordination
   2. Certification packages - Space (NASA/ESA), automotive (ISO 26262), medical (IEC 62304)
   3. Support contracts - SLA-backed assistance for production deployments
   4. Platform SaaS - Fleet-wide anomaly visibility, vitality dashboards, phantom analytics
   5. Hardware partnerships - Pre-validated Reflex on Jetson, ESP32, rad-hard platforms

   Protective measures:

   Threat             │ Defense                                                                                                   
   -------------------+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Big tech clones it │ Open core = prior art. They can use primitives, but enterprise extensions are proprietary
   Patent trolls      │ Defensive publication of core concepts. Join OIN (Open Invention Network)
   Commoditization    │ Moat is in self-organizing extensions + certification. Primitives commoditizing is fine - grows the market
   Cloud capture      │ Keep inference on-edge. No cloud dependency = no cloud lock-in by others

   Precedents that work:

   •  Redis - Open core, Redis Enterprise for clustering/security
   •  Elastic - Open search, commercial X-Pack extensions
   •  Databricks - Open Spark, commercial platform
   •  Liquid AI (CfC creators) - Research open, Liquid Foundation Models commercial

   The Reflex fits this mold: fundamental primitive is open (grows ecosystem), self-organizing intelligence is commercial (captures value).
