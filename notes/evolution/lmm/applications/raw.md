# Raw Thoughts: Applications

## Stream of Consciousness

Robotics is the throughline. Every "vertical" is just robots in context:
- Aerospace = robots in space
- Medical = robots in surgery  
- Industrial = robots in factories
- Defense = robots in conflict
- Automotive = robots on roads

This simplifies everything. One community (robotics engineers), one conference circuit, one sales motion.

But within robotics, there are distinct categories with different needs:

MANIPULATION - the hands
- Force feedback needs 10kHz to feel natural
- Collision response needs sub-ms to not hurt
- Assembly insertion needs compliance to not jam
- The Reflex enables: delicate handling, human-safe collaboration

MOBILE - the wheels/tracks
- Obstacle avoidance at speed needs sub-ms reaction
- Cliff detection needs instant wheel stop
- Dynamic balance needs continuous correction
- The Reflex enables: faster navigation, safer operation

LEGGED - the legs
- Balance recovery is the killer app
- Sub-ms center of mass correction prevents falls
- Slip response redistributes weight instantly
- The Reflex enables: rough terrain, dynamic movement

DRONES - the rotors
- Gust response needs 10kHz stabilization
- Proximity alert needs instant reaction
- Motor failure needs immediate redistribution
- The Reflex enables: stable flight in wind, collision avoidance

SURGICAL - the precision
- Tissue tracking follows heartbeat at 10kHz
- Force limiting protects tissue
- Tremor cancellation augments surgeon
- The Reflex enables: precision on moving targets

SPACE - the distance
- Light-speed delay makes Earth control impossible
- Autonomous fault recovery is survival
- Learns normal in-situ (can't simulate everything)
- The Reflex enables: true autonomy at distance

## Questions Arising

- Which application is the best beachhead?
- Are there applications we haven't thought of?
- How do we prioritize without spreading too thin?

## First Instincts

Manipulation might be the biggest market. Every robot arm needs better force control. But space is the most compelling story. Drones might be fastest to deploy.

## What Scares Me

Trying to be everything to everyone. Need to pick a lane and dominate it first.
