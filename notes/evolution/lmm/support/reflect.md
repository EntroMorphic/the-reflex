# Reflect: Support & Maintenance

## Core Insight

**Support is the relationship after the sale.**

The sale is a transaction. Support is the relationship. Relationships determine:
- Renewals (recurring revenue)
- Expansions (additional deployments)
- References (new customers)
- Reputation (market position)

Support excellence is competitive advantage, not cost center.

---

## Resolved Tensions

### Node 9 (Scaling Support)
**Resolution:** Three-layer approach.

**Layer 1: Self-service (80% of issues)**
- Documentation
- FAQs
- Community forums
- Automated diagnostics

**Layer 2: Assisted (15% of issues)**
- Standard support tickets
- Email/chat
- Scheduled calls

**Layer 3: Intensive (5% of issues)**
- Critical response
- Screen sharing
- On-site if needed

Invest heavily in Layer 1 to reduce load on Layers 2-3.

### Node 11 (Global Support)
**Resolution:** Asynchronous first, then expand.

Phase 1: US hours, async for global
Phase 2: Add EU coverage (8-hour overlap)
Phase 3: Add APAC coverage (24/7)

Scale with customer base. Don't over-invest early.

### Node 12 (Success vs Support)
**Resolution:** Clear division.

**Support:** Reactive. Fix problems. Restore function.
**Success:** Proactive. Ensure value. Drive outcomes.

Both are needed. Support keeps things working. Success ensures they're worth working.

## The Reforging Process

Reforging is regular maintenance, not emergency repair.

**Triggers:**
- Accuracy below threshold for 7+ days
- False positive rate above threshold for 7+ days
- Domain Expert observes environment change
- New anomaly types appear

**Process:**
1. Alert: Trigger detected
2. Analysis: Root cause assessment
3. Decision: Tune thresholds vs full reforge
4. Action: Threshold adjustment or new Forge cycle
5. Validation: Confirm improvement
6. Documentation: Update baseline

**Duration:** Threshold tuning = hours. Full reforge = weeks.

## What I Now Understand

Support is the long game. Initial sale is one-time. Support relationship is forever.

Every support interaction is an opportunity:
- To solve a problem (obvious)
- To learn about their environment (feed back to product)
- To deepen the relationship (trust building)
- To identify expansion (more Reflexors, more applications)

Self-service is the multiplier. Documentation that prevents one ticket saves hours. Invest accordingly.

Reforging is normalized maintenance. It's not failure; it's evolution. The world changes. Instincts must adapt. This is healthy.
